**A First Course in Real Analysis** is an open, rigorous, and readable introduction to the foundations of real analysis, intended for undergraduate mathematics students encountering the subject for the first time.

The book loosely follows the structure of Stephen Abbott's *Understanding Analysis* (2nd ed., Springer, 2015), covering the real number system, sequences and series, continuity, differentiation, and integration. It aims to present definitions and theorems clearly and concisely, with full proofs.

This book was created as a personal project to deepen my own understanding of real analysis. It is a work in progress and new content is added regularly.
