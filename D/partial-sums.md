**Definition:** Given a sequence $(a_n)$, the $n$-th **partial sum** is:

$$s_n = \sum_{k=1}^{n} a_k = a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n$$

The sequence $(s_n)$ is called the **sequence of partial sums** of $(a_n)$. The [convergence of the series](../D/convergence-series) $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n$ is defined entirely in terms of the convergence of $(s_n)$.
